The Stone Alphabet

Dear Booking Team,

I would like to inquire whether you might be interested in presenting our trio «The Stone Alphabet» with our current concert program in your concert series.

Our name reflects our concept, as we largely perform on unique stone instruments. These are prototypes based on decades of experience, academic research, and tireless innovation.

On this extraordinary sonic foundation, we've created our own sound alphabet – structured compositions with adventurous freedoms.

The Ensemble

The Stone Alphabet consists of the composer/performers:

  • Matthias Brodbeck: sounding stones, Orgalitho, drums, gongs, percussion
  • Dominik Dolega: lithophone, stone gongs, stone table, Orgalitho, aluphone, percussion
  • Mathias Steinauer: Fender Rhodes, synthesizer, quarter-tone clavinet, Orgalitho

We are touring in November 2025 and May 2026 and would be delighted to perform at your venue and captivate your audience.

"At first glance, these stone instruments with their polished or rough surfaces are beautiful, and access to the realm of stone sounds is simple - almost casually, you become friends. Simultaneously, the quiet patterns develop a pull that doesn't let concentration wander.

The music of The Stone Alphabet invites you on a journey. It's not there to impress anyone. Where this inner journey takes you is open-ended. The music offers fields - the associative images are your own."

Helena Winkelman, Swiss composer
